Artfinity

Welcome to Artfinity, the digital portfolio of our group in ICT 013: Visual Graphics Design. This website showcases our creativity, skills, and passion through various graphic design outputs we created during the semester.

This website also serves as our final examination for ICT 012: Web Development and Programming. It reflects how we've combined our design knowledge with web development skills. Our goal is to express ideas visually through posters, logos, infographics, and more.

THIS EXAMINATION IS MORE THAN A SCHOOL REQUIREMENT.

IT SHOWCASES OUR GROWTH IN BOTH VISUAL DESIGN AND WEB DEVELOPMENT.
